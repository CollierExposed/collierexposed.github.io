Key Characters

Principal figures connected to Collier and the investigation.

Stuart Collier

Murdoch tabloid enforcer — fixer, liar, and architect of smears; implicated as a key facilitator in the Daniel Morgan murder and Guppygate assaults.

Daniel Morgan

Private investigator lured to the Golden Lion in March 1987 and brutally murdered. His intention to expose police corruption made him a target.

Darius Guppy

Avenger figure who plotted violence after Collier’s blackmail of Patricia Holder; central to Guppygate.

Boris Johnson

Participant in the 1990 Guppygate call who agreed to supply Collier’s details to facilitate an assault.

Preservation & Ethics

We archive, mirror and distribute to prevent suppression. Our standards are archival, evidential and ethical.

  • Permanent Archiving: redundant backups and blockchain hashing where feasible.
  • Open Access: downloads and documents available for researchers.
  • Ethical Practice: every claim tied to records and corroboration.
